Beef Bowl

A bowl of steamed white rice topped with seasoned ground beef garnished with sliced green onion

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 cup steamed white rice
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 green onion
  • Optional - wonton strips

Directions

  1. Prepare 1 cup white rice in rice cooker.
  2. Thin slice green onion then separate green and white parts.
  3. Mince ginger and garlic separately.
  4. Mix ginger, sesame oil, soy sauce, and brown sugar in a bowl. Put aside for later.
  5. Brown meat with garlic and white part of green onion in large skillet on medium-high heat. Drain fat.
  6. Stir in sauce mix with ground beef and bring to a boil, reduce heat and let simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Serve over white rice, garnish with green onion and wonton strips.
  8. Enjoy!
