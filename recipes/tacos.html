Ground Beef Tacos

A hard shelled ground beef taco with lettuce, tomato, and shredded cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 pkg. McCormick taco seasoning
  • 12 hard taco shells or flour tortillas
  • 3/4 cup water
  • Assorted Toppings: shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, shredded cheese, salsa or sour cream

Directions

  1. Brown meat in large skillet on medium-high heat. Drain fat.
  2. Stir in seasoning mix and water.
  3. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes covered.
  4. Spoon into warm taco shells. Serve with desired toppings.
  5. Enjoy!
